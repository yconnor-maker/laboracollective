Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to Labora Collective or create a membership account, we collect the information you provide — including your name, email address, and payment details (processed securely by our payment provider). We also collect standard analytics data such as page views, device type, and referral sources to improve our editorial products.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver your subscriptions, newsletters, and membership content
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Send editorial updates and product announcements
  • Improve our publications and user experience

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers (such as Stripe) and we do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We may use analytics tools to understand how our readers engage with our content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@laboracollective.com. You can unsubscribe from any email communications using the link in any email we send.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@laboracollective.com.
