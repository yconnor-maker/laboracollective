About Roots & Wings

Roots & Wings covers the full landscape of reproductive planning — fertility treatments, egg freezing, IVF, adoption, surrogacy, and the choice not to have children at all. We trace the medical evidence, the financial realities, and the emotional weight of decisions that shape entire lives. Including the choices no one tells you are choices.
