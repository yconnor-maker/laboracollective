About Second Act

Second Act covers the health transition that medicine forgot to study and society forgot to talk about. Perimenopause, menopause, HRT decisions, bone health, cardiovascular changes, cognitive shifts, and the decades of life that follow. We refuse to treat midlife as decline — it's a second act, and it deserves real medicine.
