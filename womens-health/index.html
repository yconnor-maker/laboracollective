About the Women's Health Journal

The Women's Health journal is the Labora Collective's largest editorial vertical. It covers the clinical, personal, and systemic dimensions of women's health — from the exam room to the statehouse.

Every article uses the Labora Collective's 3-layer source transparency system: visual evidence, contextual text, and editorial analysis are separated so readers can verify claims and form their own conclusions.
