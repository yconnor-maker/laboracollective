About This Guide

Built for the moments when the system fails you

Whether you're trying to understand what the Dobbs decision means for your state, prepare for potential healthcare barriers, or build a long-term safety plan — From Crisis to Care walks you through it all, step by step.

This isn't a policy brief or a news article. It's a working document — designed to be filled out, returned to, and used when it matters most.

Why this exists

Since Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization overturned Roe v. Wade in 2022, reproductive healthcare access has changed dramatically across the United States — and the data on who bears the greatest burden of those changes is clear. This workbook was built to close the gap between what people need to know and what they actually have access to.
