Every Friday Issue

Three pillars. The week's complete intelligence picture.

Pillar I · Clinical

Clinical Intelligence Lead

The week's most important clinical development — with full translation for what it means in practice. Trial results, guideline updates, safety signals, and FDA actions that practitioners need to know before Monday.

Pillar II · Political

Political & Legal Intelligence

What moved in courts, legislatures, and agencies this week. Policy changes with clinical implications, legal developments affecting care access, and In Your District — state-level impact data for advocates.

Pillar III · Infrastructure

Infrastructure Intelligence

The systems layer — funding, workforce, technology, and care delivery infrastructure. What changed in the mechanisms that make care possible (or impossible) this week.

Briefing Structure

One read. Complete picture. Every Friday.

I

Intelligence Lead

The week's single most important development — across all three pillars. The thing you most need to know before the weekend.

II

Pillar 1 — Clinical

Full clinical coverage: trials, approvals, safety signals, guideline updates. Everything that changes what practitioners do Monday morning.

III

Pillar 2 — Political

Courts, legislatures, agencies, and executive action. The legal and political developments with direct clinical and access implications. Includes In Your District.

IV

Pillar 3 — Infrastructure

Funding, workforce, delivery systems, technology. The structural layer that determines whether the clinical and political realities above can actually be addressed.

V

Forward Look

What to watch in the next seven days. Specific dates, deadlines, rulings expected, and hearings scheduled. No vague language — actual calendar items.
