Reading the Reports Together

These domains don't exist in isolation.
The connections are the intelligence.

09 → 01 → 04
The administrative action chain: Every executive action in Domain 09 traces to a clinical or access consequence in Domain 01, mediated by the infrastructure dismantling documented in Domain 04. Read 09 first.
02 + 03
The legal-economic loop: Domain 02's legal developments produce the economic costs measured in Domain 03. The $133B annual drag is the price tag of the court cases and policy moves in Domain 02.
06 + 11
The practice gap double-bind: Domain 06 shows the science on menopause has never been better. Domain 11 shows that evidence still isn't reaching practitioners. Both domains are required to understand the full failure.
10 + 01
Highest-risk populations: Domain 10's documentation of care-avoidance among immigrant populations maps directly onto the maternal mortality disparities in Domain 01. The groups most deterred from care are the groups with the least margin for error.
How to Use These Reports

Built for three audiences. Calibrated for each.

Each report is written so that the same document can serve a department chair reviewing evidence, a campaign researcher preparing briefing materials, and a patient trying to understand what is happening to her care. The intelligence doesn't change — the translation does.

The Signal Block at the top of each report surfaces the central contradiction and three immediate implications in under two minutes. The full report is behind it — 3,500+ words of evidence, analysis, and forward look.

Clinicians & Practitioners
OB-GYNs, midwives, health policy staff

Use the Signal Block and Forward Look for quick practice translation. Use the body sections for evidence review and the "In Your District" boxes for local context. The Practice Change callouts are designed specifically for frontline providers.

Advocates & Campaign Teams
Research directors, communications staff, organizers

Domain 03 (Economic) and Domain 02 (Political) are the entry points. The State Divergence Grids show district-level impact. "In Your District" boxes are citation-ready for constituent briefings. The Forward Look gives you the calendar.

Patients & the Public
Anyone trying to understand what is happening to their care

Read the Opening Narrative of any domain. It opens with a specific patient or provider scenario before any data appears. The "What This Means Right Now" section in each Signal Block gives three immediate implications without requiring clinical background.
