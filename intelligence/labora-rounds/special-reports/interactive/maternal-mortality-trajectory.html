What Happened — Timeline

2021 Dobbs v. Jackson argued before Supreme Court. Texas SB 8 takes effect September 2021 — first near-total abortion ban. COVID-19 pandemic peaks maternal mortality across all groups.
2022 Dobbs decided June 24, 2022 — Roe v. Wade overturned. 14 states trigger immediate or near-immediate bans. OB-GYN residency applications drop 10.5% in ban states.
