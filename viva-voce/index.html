About Viva Voce

Viva Voce is the Labora Collective's interview and oral history series. It fills the gap between data-driven reporting and lived experience by giving extended space to the people whose expertise comes from doing the work — not just studying it.

Every interview is edited for clarity but published with full context. Complete transcripts are available for members.
