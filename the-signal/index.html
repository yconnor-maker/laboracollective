What is The Signal?

The Signal is a physician-led newsletter from the Labora Collective. Each edition identifies 2–3 developments in women's health — policy changes, research findings, clinical shifts — and traces them to their structural roots using our 3-layer source transparency system.

It's designed to bridge the gap between what's happening to women's health (systems, politics, economics) and what's happening in women's health (guidelines, evidence, practice).
