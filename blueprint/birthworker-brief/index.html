About The Birthworker Brief

The Birthworker Brief serves the broader birthworker community — doulas, midwives, lactation consultants, and allied professionals navigating the intersection of clinical practice, policy changes, and community-based care. News, analysis, and the professional intelligence birthworkers need to do their jobs better.
